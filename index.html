Our Process

Our process is simple and effective! First, we help you define your market through research and consultation. Next, we work with you to identify your target audience, and advise you on the best tools to reach your potential customers. We share our findings in accessible, easy to understand visual reports, and use what we learned to help you refine your marketing strategies so that you can focus on growing your business, your brand and your visibility. We want to see your business succeed! See our pricing options for a summary of the features each package includes.

Market Analysis

Our subscription-based software platform helps you analyze your market to identify possible advertizing opportunities, strategies and methodologies. It will help you have a better grasp on what your overall marketing approach should be, and where you should be spending your advertizing budget to have the biggest impact.

Custom Marketing

Our software will help you identify your current customer base, and define areas for potential market growth and expansion. This can help you determine how best to reach them, through which communications, online and social media platforms, as well as determining currently untapped markets.

Visual Reporting

Our software generates simple, attractive, accessible visual reports to help you track your advertizing spending, progress, and impact. Available in a variety of formats, these graphics and tables can be inserted into any corporate reporting and performance measurement materials.

Impact Analysis

As part of our Premium plan, we offer customized analysis of the impact of your advertizing decisions in terms of reach, response, and revenue. This can be key to helping you understand how to fine-tune your advertizing approach for future growth and further expansion.
